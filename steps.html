Step 1: Prepare the Apples

  • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and lightly grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  • Peel and slice the apples into thin wedges with an apple slicer or knife.
  • In a large bowl, toss and stir sliced sppales with lemon juice. Add the granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and flour.
  • Pour the apple mixture evenly unto the greased baking dish.

Step 2: Make the Crisp Topping

  • In a seperate bowl, combine the rolled oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Stir to blend the dry ingredients.
  • Add the cold cubbed butter to the mixture,
  • with your hands message the butter into the mixture until it creates a coarse cumble.
  • For added crunch, add in your nuts.

Step 3: Assemble and Bake

  • Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the apple filling, covering all the apples.
  • Place baking dish un the oven for 40-45 mins or until topping is golden brown.
  • Remove apple crisp from oven and allow it to cool for at least 10 mins before serving.

Step 4: Ready to Serve

  • Serve the apple crisp warm, ideally with a scoop of ice cream or a dallop of whipped cream.
  • Can also be served cold over greek yogurt.
